<!-- OVERVIEW -->
<h2 style="font-size: 1.45em; font-weight: 700; color: #111; border-bottom: 2px solid #e0eaf2; padding-bottom: 6px; margin: 36px 0 16px 0;">Overview</h2>

<p style="margin: 0 0 14px 0; font-size: 0.98em; color: #3a3a3a;">
  This challenge is an open scientific competition organized to advance machine learning methods for
  computational materials science. It is designed to be inclusive, reproducible, and fair. All
  participants are held to the same standards of scientific integrity.
</p>
<p style="margin: 0 0 14px 0; font-size: 0.98em; color: #3a3a3a;">
  These terms govern participation in the competition hosted on Codabench. They are intended to
  protect the integrity of the results and the scientific value of the challenge for all participants
  and the broader research community.
</p>

<!-- EVALUATION -->
<h2 style="font-size: 1.45em; font-weight: 700; color: #111; border-bottom: 2px solid #e0eaf2; padding-bottom: 6px; margin: 36px 0 16px 0;">Evaluation and ranking</h2>
<div style="background: #f8f9fa; border: 1px solid #dee2e6; border-radius: 10px; overflow: hidden;">
  <div style="padding: 12px 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid #eaecef; font-size: 0.94em; color: #444; line-height: 1.55;">
    The final ranking is based on Mean Absolute Error (MAE) in eV/atom on the <strong>private test set</strong>, which is never shown to participants during the competition.

  <div style="padding: 12px 16px; border-bottom: 1px solid #eaecef; font-size: 0.94em; color: #444; line-height: 1.55;">
